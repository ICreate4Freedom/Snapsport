# SnapsPort — Privacy Policy

_Last updated: July 1, 2026_

SnapsPort ("the app," "we," "us") helps you move your Snapchat Memories from a
Snapchat data-export ZIP file into your iPhone's photo library. This policy
explains exactly what the app does and does not do with your data.

## The short version

- Your photos and videos are processed **entirely on your device**. They are never
  uploaded to us or to any server we control.
- We do **not** require an account, and we do **not** operate any backend server that
  receives your memories.
- The only data that leaves your device is what's required to process your **in-app
  purchase**, handled by Apple and by our payments provider, RevenueCat.

## What the app accesses on your device

- **Your selected Snapchat export ZIP file(s).** The app reads and unzips these locally
  to find your photos and videos, then saves them to your photo library. The extracted
  files are stored temporarily in the app's cache and deleted after saving.
- **Photo library (add) permission.** Used solely to save your memories into your Camera
  Roll or a "SnapsPort" album. The app does not read or scan your existing photos.
- **Local notifications.** If you switch away from the app during a long extraction, the
  app schedules a local "extraction complete" notification. This is generated on-device;
  no notification data is sent anywhere.

## Data collected through in-app purchases

To sell the one-time "Unlock All Memories" purchase, we use **RevenueCat** as our
purchase-management provider, and **Apple** processes the actual payment.

- **Purchase history** — records of whether the unlock was purchased, so your purchase can
  be restored across your devices.
- **A randomly generated identifier** (a RevenueCat anonymous app-user ID / device
  identifier). This is **not** your name or email; it is used only to associate a purchase
  with the app installation and to restore purchases.

We do **not** collect your name, email address, contacts, location, or the contents of
your photos. RevenueCat's handling of purchase data is governed by their privacy policy:
https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

Apple's handling of payment information is governed by Apple's privacy policy.

## Data we do NOT collect

- No photos, videos, or their contents are transmitted off your device.
- No account, email, name, phone number, or contacts.
- No advertising identifiers, and no third-party analytics or ad SDKs.
- No location data.

## Children

SnapsPort is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from
them.

## Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. Material changes will be reflected by the
"Last updated" date above.
